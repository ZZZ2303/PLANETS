Saturn

Saturn is the sixth planet in the solar system and it's the second largest planet in the solar system
and it's the second planet in the outer planets too, it has seven beautiful rings around it made
out of chunks of ice and rock and although it's not the only one that has rings but it's the only one that has
rings as beautiful and complicated as saturn's. Like jupiter ,saturn is made mostly of hydrogen and helium.
Saturn takes 10.7 hours to make a full orbit around itself and 29 earth years to make one orbit around the sun.
Saturn has 53 known moons and 29 additional moons which is 82 moons in total.
