Mars (The Red Planet)

Mars is the fourth planet to the sun and it's the last one of the inner planets which is a cold ,dusty, desert
with a very thin atmosphere, Mars is also smaller than earth, Its days are a little longer than earth days (24 hours)
but it takes 687 earth days to take a full orbit around the sun. It's also a rocky planet full with volcanoes, winds ,and
chemical reactions, the atmosphere contains co2 ,argon, nitrogen, and a small amount of oxygen and water vapour.
Mars also has no rings but it has two moons named Phobos and Deimos.It's called the red planet because its
soil contains iron minerals causing the soil and atmosphere to look red.
